AMaR: Autonomous Multi-Utility Rover

 We are building a rover in RTC. We intended to develop a 6-wheel rover that could discover the quickest path given a destination and be equipped with various sensors. The curiosity rover model inspired us. We knew we'd have to use reinforcement learning to train this. We opted to begin with Carla and go to more powerful simulations such as NVIDIA Isaac Sim when we have more resources.
